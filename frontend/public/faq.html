Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about SmartWrite's features and capabilities.

General Questions

What file formats does SmartWrite support?
SmartWrite currently supports PDF files. We process both text-based and scanned PDFs using advanced OCR technology.
Is there a file size limit?
Yes, the maximum file size is 50MB per document. For larger files, please contact our support team.
How secure are my documents?
We use industry-standard encryption and security measures. Your documents are encrypted both in transit and at rest.

Account & Billing

What subscription plans are available?
We offer Free, Pro, and Enterprise plans. Each plan comes with different features and processing limits.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your access will continue until the end of your billing period.

Technical Support

How can I get technical support?
You can reach our support team through live chat, email, or by submitting a ticket through your dashboard.
What are the system requirements?
SmartWrite works in any modern web browser. We recommend using the latest version of Chrome, Firefox, or Safari.
